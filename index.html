Frequently Asked Questions

Parents often ask us these questions — find your answer here

What are the batch timings? ▼
We offer multiple batch timings to suit school schedules — Morning (7 AM – 9 AM), Afternoon (1 PM – 3 PM), and Evening (5 PM – 7 PM). You can choose the batch that fits your child's school time when you enroll.
What is the batch size? ▼
We strictly maintain a maximum of 15 students per batch. This ensures every student gets personal attention from the teacher, and no child is ignored or left behind.
What are the monthly fees? ▼
Our fees are very reasonable and affordable for families in Supaul. Fees vary by class level (6–8, 9–10, or 11–12) and the number of subjects. Please call us at 7454957454 or visit the institute for the latest fee structure.
Is study material provided? ▼
Yes! We provide specially prepared printed notes, chapter-wise question banks, and practice papers for all subjects. Students don't need to buy expensive books from outside.
Do you take doubt classes? ▼
Absolutely. We hold dedicated doubt-clearing sessions every week. Students can also approach teachers before or after class for any help. We believe no doubt should remain unsolved.
Which subjects are taught? ▼
We cover all core subjects: Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Science, English, Hindi for Class 6–10. For Class 11–12, we offer Science stream (PCM / PCB) and Commerce stream (Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies).
Can I visit before enrolling? ▼
Yes, parents and students are welcome to visit our institute any day between 9 AM and 6 PM. You can meet the teachers, see the classrooms, and ask all your questions before deciding. No appointment needed!

About Samarpan Classes

Our Story – Built on a Simple Promise

Samarpan Classes was founded with one simple goal: every child in Supaul deserves quality education that is affordable, personal, and result-oriented.

We started small, with just a handful of students and a deep belief that the right teacher can change a student's life. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted coaching institutes in Supaul, Bihar — and we're proud of every student who has walked out of our classrooms with confidence and great marks.

Our teachers are not just subject experts — they are mentors who understand the pressure students face and guide them with patience and care. We work closely with parents to ensure every child is on track.
